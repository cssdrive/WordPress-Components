We're pleased to announce that our "Commission-free deposits" special offer has been extended! Through the 31st of August, 2018, we will compensate the entire commission amount on account deposits made via payment systems. To take part in this offer, simply top up your account via one of the payment systems listed on our website during the offer period.
 
 
We'd also like to remind you of the following:
 
Commission fees for bank transfers are withheld by the sending bank and will not be compensated.
 
Commission fees for transfers via partners (paying agents) are also not compensated.
 
Alpari is only compensating the commission fees charged by payment systems. Any additional commission fees will not be compensated.
